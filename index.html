Jakub

My design approach is grounded in three pillars:

QUALITY
EFFICIENCY
INNOVATION

Quality is the foundation—no matter the project, execution, or format. Today's industry demands processes that are both efficient and effective, and I believe in elevating those processes through innovation—in both tools and mindset.

Working vendor-side, keeping pace with agency life, and spending years within an in-house creative team have each added depth to my experience. From mastering the craft of print production to adapting the speed and strategy of agency work in the age of AI, and navigating the dynamics of corporate organizations, I've developed a well-rounded perspective. It allows me to connect the dots at scale-whether leading a focused sprint or collaborating across complex structures.

Beyond design, I've had the privilege of mentoring and inspiring emerging creative talent. By fostering learning opportunities and giving designers visibility into the broader business, I've helped them grow stronger, more thoughtful, and more confident in their craft.
